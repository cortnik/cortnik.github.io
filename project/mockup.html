WOMEN + RIYADH METRO 2018

How well does the proposed metro serve

university women in Riyadh?

BACKGROUND

In a city where women cannot drive, a public rail and bus system could have a tremendous impact on the mobility of women.

We have analyzed movement to/from Riyadh's women's university, Princess Noura University, using Origin-Destination matrices derived from cell phone call detail records by the Center for Complex Engineering Systems.

Our analysis identifies "hotspots" where women travel before or after going to the Princess Noura campus. In addition, we've calculated likely shortests paths to/from these hotspots and located service gaps in the proposed rail system based on the hotspots.
